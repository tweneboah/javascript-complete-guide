CONFIGURE CURRENCY
  • From your Shopify admin panel you need to go to Settings > General
  • Scroll down to the Store currency block, select default currency and save preset. Set the default currency for your shop and click "SAVE"
  • In the Store Currency block click the Change formatting button.Click on the CURRENCY FORMATTING option and add the red html code below:

    <span class=money> your currency *Do not change red texts </span>

ADD NEW CURRENCY IN THEME. WORK WITH PREBUILD CUSTOM METHOD

Please be informed that you may add a new currency in the Header section

  • Go to the Online Store on your Admin Panel, go to the Themes section and click on the Customize button.
  • On the top of the Customizer page, choose Section tool and click on the Header
  • Scroll to the bottom of the screen click on the Add content button and choose the Multiple currencies option.
  • On the Multiple Currencies page you need to fill in the Currence you wish to support field.

    You may configure your shipping settings through the “Settings” page, choosing the “Shipping” option. To enable your shipping calculator, we recommend you to configure your shipping rates with the help of the guide below:

    SET UP SHIPPING RATES - OFFICIAL DOCUMENTATION

    • Instruction:

      Before you start to set it up, please note:

      • When your store has only one-way shipping - it will work as a standard;
      • In case, you have two or more ways shipping - you will have a “SHIPPING & HANDLING” message

        • To edit this message, please follow the instructions below:

        To edit this message, please follow the instructions below:

      • Access the “Online Store” page, select the “Thems “ option and click on the drop-down menu “Actions”
      • Choose “Edit language” option
      • Select “Cart” and scroll the “Shipping variants” field.
      • If Shipping variants field is empty: SHIPPING & HANDLING field is hidden.

    Kindly note, if the “Shipping variants” field is empty, the “SHIPPING & HANDLING” field will b hidden.
